Snake Game

Controls

Use the arrow keys to control the snake.

Collect the red food to grow and increase your score.

Avoid hitting the walls or yourself!

Press 'R' to restart after game over.

Speed Settings

Click the speed buttons or use number keys to change speed:

  • Slow (1): For beginners
  • Medium (2): Default speed
  • Fast (3): For experts

About This Game

Classic Snake game where you control a snake to eat food and grow without hitting walls or yourself. Features three difficulty levels with adjustable speed settings for players of all skill levels.
