About This Game

The classic Tetris game with a vibrant, sparkly twist! Arrange falling blocks to create complete lines and score points. Features three difficulty levels, colorful visuals, and special effects when clearing lines. The game now includes a beautiful starry background, hold piece functionality, and improved visual design!
